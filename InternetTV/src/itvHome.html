Welcome

What Is InternetTV?

InternetTV is a next-generation social media site designed to maximize the quality of user experience via a unique combination of simplicity and customizability.

How iTV Differs

InternetTV differs from the current industry leaders first and foremost by placing the site's users above financial interests. This philosophy results in a sleek and clean experience unburdened by excessive advertisements and other nonsense which users frequently complain about and upper management rarely does anything about.

Internet TV is all about offering high quality experiences to the masses who actually use the platform. This means simplicity for a quick learning curve as well as customizability so each person's experiences is as much to their individual liking as possible. Every feature gets carefully considered for balance between simplicity and customizability to ensure the most important core features remain well-executed and the extent of customization stays within the range of individualistic views of beautiful elegance and far away from cluttered bloat.

The Internet TV Business Model

InternetTV utilizes advertisement systems unlike anything currently available on mainstream sites. Where as the current model tends to be all about forcing undesirable ads onto content viewers and paying tiny fractions of ad revenue to content producers, the iTV model strives to meet the desires of content viewers, content producers, advertisers, and iTV employees alike; with the majority of ad revenue going to the producers making iTV what is is and only a small minority of that ad revenue going to executives. The theory being that by placing the needs of the masses of users, producers, advertisers and iTV employees above the greed of executives that far more high-quality content will be produced as the result of people actually having legit incentives to motivate them to put in greater thought and effort into their works knowing that they will actually be rewarded based according to the value they provide.

Specifically, the advertisement systems works like this:

  • Advertisements negotiated producer:advertiser directly
  • All ads display skip button after 3 seconds
  • Ads can be paused/fastforwarded/rewound/etc just like standard videos
  • Ad info + link button visible up top on video pause
  • 90%:10% profit split producer:InternetTV
  • Ads can be placed anywhere from start to end of each video
  • Which ads play when && where can be chosen manually or algorithmically
  • Ads can be viewed in each profile's "Ad library"
  • Ad revenue based on time watched (checks for unique IP/account ID)
